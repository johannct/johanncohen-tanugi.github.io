About Me

I am a senior researcher at the Laboratoire de Physique de Clermont (LPCA), affiliated with CNRS and Université Clermont Auvergne. My research currently focuses on observational cosmology, mostly in the context of the Vera C. Rubin Observatory and its Dark Energy Science Collaboration (DESC).

Previously, I worked at the Laboratoire Univers et Particules de Montpellier (LUPM) mainly within the Fermi LAT collaboration.
